My Top 5 Movies of 2024

SupaCell

This is a movie about some people with supernatural powers, their abilities are as a result of a mutation from the Sickel cell from one of their parents.
They are also being hunted by a group who experiment on their kinds.

Dune (Part Two)

This is an interesting Sci-Fi/action blockbuster, Even better than the first film, the stakes are exponentially higher, the power struggles even more mythic, the on-screen world-building richer and more exotically filigreed, and the visuals even more dazzling.

Furiosa

Snatched from the Green Place of Many Mothers, young Furiosa falls into the hands of a great biker horde led by the warlord Dementus. Sweeping through the Wasteland, they come across the Citadel, presided over by the Immortan Joe.
As the two tyrants fight for dominance, Furiosa soon finds herself in a nonstop battle to make her way home.

Deadpool & Wolverine

Deadpool & Wolverine is a 2024 American superhero film based on Marvel Comics featuring the characters Deadpool and Wolverine. Here we all thought we would not be seen Wolverine agan. Produced by Marvel Studios.

The Painter

In this movie a retired CIA agent paints as a hobby. But his past has come back to haunt him, as a mysterious woman he met somewhere shows up. He must find the way to evade the incoming threat in forms of some trained op recruits
